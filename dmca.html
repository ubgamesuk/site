Last updated - 10th April 2025

How to file a complaint

If you are a copyright owner, or authorised to act on behalf of the copyright owner, and believe that content available on ubgames.uk, ubmini.com or any of our externally provided links infringes your copyright, please submit a written notice of copyright infringement to our team with the following information.

Identification

Identification of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed. Please provide sufficient detail (e.g. the title or URL) so we can locate it on the site.

Information

Your contact information, including your name, address, telephone number and email address.

Statement and signature

A statement that the information in the notification is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, that you are authorised to act on behalf of the copyright owner, plus an electronic or physical signature of the copyright owner.

Submission of your notice

Please send your notice via email at contact@ubgames.uk - we will remove any infringed content as soon as possible.
